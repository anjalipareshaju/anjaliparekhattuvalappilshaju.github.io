About Me

I am a doctoral student in the Department of Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru. My research focuses on soil moisture dynamics, hydrological modeling, and the validation of remote-sensing products under the REWARD program. I have previously completed my M.Tech in Water Resources Engineering (minor in Geotechnical Engineering) at IISc with a CGPA of 9.3/10.
